quick memo from naming chat

Use ID10 for now. If facility people later want BL-ID10, handle that in rendering.

Areas we think we know:
IVU should be PTL.
DCM and the major optics should be OH.
White beam slit should be OH.
Sample stage and detector are user side, probably EH.
Movable mask location was not checked. If unsure, assume PTL and mark it.

For white beam slit, do not use SLIT in the token. I can look like 1.
Use WBSLT. Keep old blade words short: top, bot, inb, outb, hgap, vgap.

For IVU girder, keep IVU as the device and put girder_x, girder_y, etc. in the final part.

Status/readback/alarm stuff is not settled yet. Do not silently convert those as if they were motors.
